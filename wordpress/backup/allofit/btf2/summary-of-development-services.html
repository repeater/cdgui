Summary of Development Services

At BreakThru Fundraising we understand that if a non-profit is underfunded, fundraising may be the symptom, but there may be other contributing factors that need to be addressed in order to become fully funded. We offer a wide range of services to assist faith-based and community non-profit organization, foundations, churches, and donors. We provide fundraising coaching, fundraising consulting, and fundraising training in the following areas:

  1. Fundraising and Development
  2. Board Development
  3. Executive / Team Leadership Development
  4. Organizational Development
  5. Marketing & Branding
  6. Strategy Planning and Execution
  7. Growth and Change Management
